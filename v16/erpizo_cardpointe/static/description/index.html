Overview

Elevate your Odoo experience with Cardpointe Payment Provider. Integrate the Cardpointe payment option into your Odoo Website and Backend, enabling smooth customer transactions and effortless refunds directly from your backend interface. Enjoy flexibility with payment acceptance on Odoo's checkout page or through redirection to the provider payment page. Plus, effortlessly manage and track all transaction details within the Odoo backend for enhanced convenience and efficiency.

Features

Seamless Integration: Easily add Cardpointe payment to your Odoo Website and Backend for smooth transactions.

Flexible Payment Options: Accept payments on Odoo's checkout page or redirect to the provider payment page.

Effortless Refunds: Initiate refunds directly from your Odoo Backend interface.

Centralized Transaction Management: Track and manage all transaction details within Odoo for enhanced efficiency..
