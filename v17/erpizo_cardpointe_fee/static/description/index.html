Cardpointe Payment Fee.

Cardpointe Fee Payment that facilitates the additional fee both front-end and back-end payment processing.

Explore this module

Overview Key Features Setup & Configuration Screenshots

Overview

An additional fee will be added

Features

Seamless Integration: Easily add Cardpointe payment fee to your cardpointe payment transactions.

Fee Enable Payment: Enable the fee from payment provider form and choose the payment fee product.

Effortless Fee Payment: Just enable the fee and during backend and front end transactions you can see additional payment fee.

Setup & Configuration

Configuration

Enable the fee from the payment provider form view.

Screenshots

Add additional fee of 3% on cardpointe website payment by Choosing Card or ACH payment modes to complete your transactions.
